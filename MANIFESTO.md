# Manifesto

## Why Was This Created?

I wanted to tell stories. I wanted people to be engulfed in my worlds, my characters, my own lore. I've been writing for ages now — fan fictions, stories, blogs, books, or sometimes even very big and long documentations for projects ;D 

And I still love writing. I still love pouring words onto the blank canvas in Word... But I also see that there is more there than just being very specific about the use of *this* word over *that*.

## AI Is a Tool

AI is a tool. It's good at being a tool. And generative AI is very good at being a generative tool — it knows how to create words, word by word, and then build sentences... This is a language model in the end :D

## So Let's Use It

Let's use this tool to write words for us. Not stories — just words. Let us create worlds. Let us create compelling characters. Let's create plot twists. Let's create dark themes. Let's indulge ourselves in worlds where cars drive next to dinosaurs, or where flames are cold as ice... Where elves still continue their journey even after defeating the Demon Lord, or some random Jo's are trying to find some steel balls ;)

## The Bottom Line

Use Inkai as a tool. Nothing more.
